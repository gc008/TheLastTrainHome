Making your way down the tunnel, your flashlight spots a handcar, and adjacent to it is an open door with a staircase leading up. Next to the door reads a sign that says "Metropolitan Museum of Art - Maintenence Entrance." You climb the stairs, knowing you're close behind the suspect because you can hear the telltale sound of drilling.

Reaching the top, you catch the mastermind burglar off guard. He never stood a chance.

In his confession, he reveals his master plan to attempt to steal a famous painting of a train out from under the nose of an ongoing auction at the Met.
